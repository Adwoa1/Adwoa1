About me

A small introduction about me

Adwoa Manu

Soon-to-be Engineer & Photographer from New Yok

I was born in Ghana and came to the States in 2012. I was immediately put in a 2nd year class at St. Catharine Academy. In a few weeks, I was introduced to a class with a teacher who defined my career today. I sarted Robotics in high school, pioneered a team of all-girls Robotics team and went on to study mechanical engineering in college.

Although, my interests in Robotics diminished as I was exposed to soo many fields in engineering, I will always credit my career in engineering to the spark my Robotics teacher ignited in me. The story today is that my interests are in green building design and renewable energy.
